Plentiful purple beetroot potato salad

Though the recipe is unique, this image is originally from NYT Cooking

Light, flavorful beetroot potato salad

I'll be honest with you: potato salad was never my thing. Despite growing up in the grey area between the midwest and the south that is southern Missouri, I could never really wrap my head around the appeal of it.

Years later and halfway around the world, my partner introduced me to this delicious purple medley, and I was baffled by how delicious I found it. The beets bring a deeper and richer flavor to the dish and, along with the vegan mayo, avoid the overtly creamy vibes of the traditional dish.

Served cold, this dish is a great complement to a hearty, warm entree, or it can be packed along with other light dishes for a picnic. Let's get into it.

Ingredients

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 2 cucumbers
  • 4 pickled cucumbers
  • 50ml pickle juice
  • 350 grams vegan mayo
  • 20 grams yellow mustard
  • 20 grams agave nectar
  • 1 tablespoon dried dill
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper


Steps

*To save time, preheat over to 170 degrees celsius

  1. Peel and cut the beets and potato into 1cm cubes.
  2. Season with salt and pepper then drizzle with oil.
  3. After covering with foil, roast for 30mins at 170 degrees celsius.
  4. Thinly slice the cucumbers and finely chop the pickles.
  5. Mix everything together after the roasted vegetables are cooled down.
